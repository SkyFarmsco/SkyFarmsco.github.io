About SkyFarms

SkyFarms is transforming how cities produce and consume food. Leveraging vertical farming, hydroponics, aeroponics, and renewable energy, we drastically reduce water use, minimize land footprint, and deliver pesticide-free, nutrient-rich produce year-round.

90% Water Savings

By using hydroponics & aeroponics, we conserve up to 90% more water than traditional farming.

Year-Round Freshness

Our climate-controlled environments ensure consistent yield and quality every season.

Circular Economy

We recycle organic waste into nutrient-rich fertilizers, closing the loop for sustainable growth.

Our Technology

At SkyFarms, technology is at the heart of everything we do. From AI-driven sensors that optimize plant growth, to IoT-enabled environmental controls, we harness data to maximize yields, minimize inputs, and enhance energy efficiency.

AI & IoT Sensors

Real-time monitoring of humidity, temperature, and nutrients for consistent quality.

Renewable Energy

Solar panels, mirrors, and efficient LED lighting minimize our carbon footprint.

Circular Nutrient Cycles

Upcycling organic waste and using natural CO₂ exchange enhance plant growth and sustainability.

Our Solutions

We provide modular vertical farming units, consulting services, and supply contracts to businesses, developers, and culinary destinations seeking fresh, local, and eco-friendly produce.

Modular Farm Units

Scalable vertical farms that fit seamlessly into urban landscapes: rooftops, basements, or stand-alone facilities.

Consulting & Integration

We advise property developers, hotels, and retail chains on integrating sustainable farming solutions on-site.

Premium Produce Supply

Direct-to-restaurant and subscription-based deliveries ensuring the freshest produce at competitive prices.
